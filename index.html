    How it works

    1. The app asks for permission to access location — allow it for accurate alerts.
    2. When you tap "Send Alert" the app obtains your location and prepares messages.
    3. The app opens your email/SMS/WhatsApp client with a prefilled message — tap send to notify your contacts.

    Note: SMS/WhatsApp/email opening depends on device and browser. On desktops some features (SMS) may open apps installed on your machine.
